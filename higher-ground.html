Recharge aims to give teens somewhere chill to go on a Friday night. This is not the kind of activity with a strict schedule, but instead a time where we hang out and see what we can come up with. The only things that are guaranteed during Recharge are a trip to Bolla or Walgreens, and some random funny game or idea. Sometimes we decide to make a spontaneous remixed music video, or maybe play knockout. No matter what we have a good time with good people on a night where there are many other "not so good" options for teens.
