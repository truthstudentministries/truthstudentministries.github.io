What's the Point

No one can argue with the TRUTH. TSM exists to spread the good news and TRUTH of Jesus Christ. We aim to help students understand that TRUTH in their lives. We build relationships with teenagers from High School and Junior High School in order to help them grow closer to Christ. We do this with missional activities, but also service, outreach, and fun activities as well.

Instilling Biblical values in teenagers is one of the most important gifts you can give them. We understand that parents play the biggest role in this process, so we want to support parents and this process in every activity we put together.
